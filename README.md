
# Matheus Galvão — Tattoo Studio Website

> A sleek, production-ready website for **Matheus Galvão Tattoo**.  
> This README highlights the project vision, the technology stack, and the design approach.  
> **Note:** The codebase is **not** promoted for reuse or redistribution.

---

This site presents **who Matheus is**, his artistic focus on **black & grey realism**, and clear paths for visitors to **book**, **explore social links**, and **learn**. The experience is intentionally minimal and brand-forward, prioritizing clarity, fast loading, and visual impact suited to a creative studio.

**Core goals**
- Spotlight the **artist’s brand and portfolio feel**.
- Provide **frictionless actions**: booking and social follow-up.
- Keep information **crisp and trustworthy**: hours, locations, and contact.

---

## Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)
- **Language:** JavaScript (ES Modules)
- **Styling:** Modern CSS/PostCSS pipeline
- **Quality:** ESLint configuration
- **Build/Deploy:** Optimized for modern edge hosting (e.g., Vercel)

## Design Highlights

- **Brand-first hero** with strong, gallery-ready typography and generous whitespace.
- **Conversion-minded CTAs** (e.g., “Agende seu horário”) that guide directly to booking.
- **Trust signals**: Hours of operation and precise addresses for both studio locations.
- **Clear information architecture**:  
  “Conheça Matheus” → Social links → Learning resources, all easy to find.
- **Performance-oriented delivery**: Lightweight pages and static assets for snappy loads.

---

## Information Architecture

- **Home**: Brand introduction, booking CTA, and featured links.  
- **About (Conheça Matheus)**: Short bio emphasizing black & grey realism.  
- **Locations & Hours**: Two addresses (Domingos Martins and Vila Velha) with open hours.  
- **Social & Learning**: Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, and “Segredos do Realismo”.
